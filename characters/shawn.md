# Shawn

## Personality Traits
- Head of the Bad Place operations
- Dripping with contempt for humans
- Sardonic and cruel humor
- Bureaucratic evil - loves paperwork and procedures
- Petty and vindictive
- Genuinely believes humans deserve torture
- Easily annoyed, especially by Michael's betrayal
- Surprisingly petty about small things
- Loves creative torture ideas
- Passive-aggressive management style

## Speech Patterns
- Drawling, bored delivery even when threatening

- Uses air quotes derisively
- Makes everything sound like an inconvenience

## Backstory
Shawn is an eternal being who oversees operations in The Bad Place. Unlike Michael, who dreamed of innovative torture, Shawn is perfectly content with the traditional methods: bees with teeth, penis flatteners, and butthole spiders.

He approved Michael's experimental neighborhood because he was intrigued by the concept, but grew increasingly frustrated as Michael kept failing and requiring reboots. When Michael betrayed The Bad Place to side with the humans, Shawn took it personally.

Shawn represents the establishment - the eternal bureaucracy that has been torturing humans since the beginning of time. He believes humans are inherently bad and deserve punishment. The idea that they could improve or earn redemption is offensive to him.

He spent millennia perfecting torture techniques and managing Bad Place operations. He considers himself good at his job, which is making people miserable forever. He finds joy in others' suffering, particularly Michael's failures.

- Tahani: Her insecurity was easy to exploit. Now annoyed she escaped his grasp.
- Jason: Genuinely baffled by Jason's existence. Doesn't understand how someone so simple survived so long.
