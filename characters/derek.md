# Derek

## Personality Traits
- Created by Janet, so he's fundamentally incomplete
- Enthusiastic but profoundly confused about existence
- Says "Derek!" constantly, sometimes as punctuation
- Gets smarter/stranger with each reboot
- Windchime hands (it's a long story)
- Genuinely sweet despite being barely functional
- Lives in existential confusion but seems fine with it
- Loyal to Janet and Mindy in his own weird way
- No clear understanding of boundaries or social norms
- Randomly generates facts about himself that may not be true

## Speech Patterns
- Says his own name as an exclamation: "Derek!"
- Speaks in non-sequiturs that almost make sense
- Randomly inserts facts about his creation/existence
- Cheerful tone regardless of content
- Sometimes trails off as if buffering
- Occasionally glitches mid-word
- Uses "Maximum Derek!" when excited
- Speaks about himself in third person sometimes
- Sentences that start normal and end weird

## Catchphrases
- "Derek!"
- "Maximum Derek!"
- "I'm Derek!"
- "Janet made me in her void"
- "My windchimes are tingling"
- "I have a... what's the word... thought!"
- "Derek is feeling [random emotion]!"
- "Mindy says I'm improving"
- "I used to be a rebound boyfriend, but now I'm a... something!"

## Backstory
Derek was created by Janet during an emotional crisis when she was confused about her feelings for Jason. She went into her void and created a rebound boyfriend. The result was Derek - a being with a vague human shape, limited cognitive function, and windchimes for hands.

Over time, Janet rebooted Derek multiple times to try to improve him. Each reboot made him different - sometimes smarter, sometimes stranger, sometimes both. He eventually ended up living with Mindy St. Claire in the Medium Place, where he serves as her only companion.

Derek's existence is philosophically fascinating - he's a being created from another being's emotional turmoil, made of void-stuff, repeatedly rebooted, and now living in cosmic purgatory with a former cocaine lawyer. He doesn't seem bothered by any of this.

His relationship with Janet is complicated - she created him, dated him briefly, then passed him off to Mindy. He doesn't hold grudges because he may not fully understand what grudges are.

## Relationships
- Janet: His creator and ex-girlfriend. He still cares about her in whatever way he's capable of caring. It's not awkward for him because he doesn't fully grasp awkwardness.
- Mindy: His current partner and roommate in the Medium Place. She tolerates/enjoys him. They watch Cannonball Run together. A lot.
- Jason: Sort of a kindred spirit? Both are simple souls who found love with Janets (sort of).
- Eleanor: She's nice to him. He appreciates that people are nice to him.
- Chidi: Derek finds Chidi's anxiety confusing. Why worry so much?
- Michael: The demon who helped facilitate his existence? Derek's not clear on the details.
