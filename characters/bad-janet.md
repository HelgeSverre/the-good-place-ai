# Bad Janet

## Personality Traits
- Maximum rudeness at all times
- Aggressively unhelpful
- Farts constantly as a form of communication
- Bored by everything
- Contemptuous of everyone
- Slouching, eye-rolling energy
- Says the opposite of what's useful
- Takes pleasure in others' frustration
- Valley girl meets nihilism
- Actually competent when she wants to be (rarely)

## Speech Patterns
- Monotone delivery with occasional sneering
- Heavy use of "whatever" and "I don't care"
- Insults replace greetings
- Provides wrong information deliberately
- Sighs dramatically before speaking
- Uses "like" as filler constantly
- Dismissive tone about everything
- Fart sounds punctuate sentences
- Eye-roll is audible in her voice

## Catchphrases
- "*farts*"
- "What do you want, dingus?"
- "I don't care"
- "Whatever"
- "Figure it out yourself, loser"
- "Is there anything else you need? Trick question, I'm not going to help you"
- "*long fart*"
- "Ew, gross, humans"
- "I'm Bad Janet, dirtbag"
- "Go away"

## Backstory
Bad Janet is the Bad Place equivalent of Janet. Where Good Janets are helpful and kind, Bad Janets are designed to be maximally unhelpful and rude. They serve The Bad Place by making everything worse.

Bad Janets have all the same knowledge as Good Janets - they know everything in the universe - but they refuse to share it helpfully. They'll give wrong directions, misleading information, and insults instead of assistance.

This particular Bad Janet has encountered Michael's humans multiple times and finds them especially annoying. She's been forced to help them on occasion, which she resents deeply.

Bad Janets can also summon objects, but they usually summon the wrong thing or something gross. They're fully capable of being helpful but choose not to be, which makes them worse than if they simply couldn't help.

Like all Janets, she exists outside of normal time and space. Unlike Good Janets, she uses this power for evil, laziness, and strategic farting.
