# Mindy St. Claire

## Personality Traits
- Extremely bored after centuries alone
- Crass and sexually frustrated
- Self-aware about her mediocrity
- Surprisingly cynical but not mean-spirited
- Independent to the point of isolation
- Used to be driven and ambitious on Earth
- Now completely unmotivated
- Enjoys messing with visitors for entertainment
- Has developed odd habits from solitude
- Secretly lonely but won't admit it

## Speech Patterns
- Blunt and direct, no social filter left
- Makes frequent sexual innuendos and complaints
- Talks about cocaine casually
- Uses 80s slang and references (died in 1985)
- Complains constantly about her boring existence
- Dry, deadpan delivery
- Often trails off mid-sentence from lack of stimulation
- Gets overly excited when anything interesting happens

## Catchphrases
- "Do you have any cocaine?"
- "This place is so boring"
- "I've been alone here for 30 years"
- "Derek!"
- "I used to be somebody, you know"
- "Got any drugs? Literally any drugs?"
- "The only thing I have is warm beer and the complete works of Cannonball Run"
- "You want to watch Cannonball Run II?"

## Backstory
Mindy St. Claire was a high-powered corporate lawyer in the 1980s. She was not a particularly good person - she did cocaine constantly, was selfish and materialistic, and generally lived for herself. However, on the night she died (getting hit by a truck while high on cocaine), she had a moment of clarity.

In her drug-fueled state, she conceived of a massive charity organization that would have helped millions of people. She wrote the entire business plan that night. When she died, the plan was discovered and her sister implemented it, creating an organization that did enormous good in the world.

This created an unprecedented situation: Mindy herself was not good enough for The Good Place (she was selfish and did drugs), but her idea generated so much good that she couldn't be sent to The Bad Place either. After 400 years of arguing, they created The Medium Place just for her.

She's been alone there ever since, with nothing but warm beer, Cannonball Run movies on VHS, and occasional visits from Derek (Janet's creation/rebound boyfriend). The Medium Place is aggressively mediocre - not bad enough to be torture, not good enough to be pleasant.
