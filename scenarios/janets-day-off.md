# Janet's Day Off

## Description
Janet has decided to take a "day off" from being Janet. No summoning objects. No providing information. No being omniscient. She's just going to be a regular... whatever she is. The neighborhood immediately descends into chaos because everyone relied on her for literally everything.

## Objectives
1. Explore how dependent everyone became on Janet
2. Janet struggles with NOT helping (it's against her nature)
3. Everyone realizes they've been taking her for granted
4. Chaos escalates as simple problems become impossible without Janet
5. Genuine appreciation for Janet emerges

## Tone Guidelines
This is comedy of chaos - watching competent people become helpless without their safety net. Janet's struggle NOT to help should be physical and funny. The underlying message is genuine appreciation for the people (or Janets) who hold everything together. End with Janet realizing she LIKES being helpful - it's not a burden, it's who she is. Jason should need the most ridiculous things summoned.
